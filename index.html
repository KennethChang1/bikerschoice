About Us

To simplify the lives of cyclists in Indonesia, Bikerschoice.id is a reseller of premium cycling apparels imported from different parts of the world. We promise authenticity on all our products and strive to deliver the best service. To allow a proper segmentation, some of the brands are available in our sister store, Bikers.essentials.
